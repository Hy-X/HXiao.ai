Help

Questions, corrections, and collaboration.

Getting in touch

For questions about an article, factual corrections, or general feedback, the best path is the GitHub issue tracker. Opening an issue creates a transparent record and makes it easier to track follow-ups.

Article corrections

If you spot an error — technical, factual, or typographic — please file an issue with the article title and the specific passage. Corrections are taken seriously and addressed promptly.

Collaboration

If you're working on something at the intersection of AI, seismology, or scientific computing and think there's a story worth writing together, reach out via the issue tracker with a brief description of the idea.

Urgent matters

For privacy or legal concerns, refer to the Privacy and Terms pages. Response times for non-urgent requests may vary.
