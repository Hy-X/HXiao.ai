Seismology workflows combine waveform processing, cataloging, modeling, and interpretation. Agentic AI can support this by orchestrating tools, tracking intermediate outputs, and preserving reproducible traces.

Instead of replacing experts, well-scoped agents reduce repetitive load: event triage, metadata checks, and literature mapping.

From Signals to Scientific Decisions

The key value is structured assistance across stages: ingestion, quality control, feature extraction, and report drafting. Human review remains central for interpretation and accountability.

Designing for Trust

Research-grade agentic systems need explicit provenance, versioned prompts, and deterministic tool logs. These practices help teams audit conclusions and strengthen scientific reliability.
